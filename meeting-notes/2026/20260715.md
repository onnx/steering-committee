### Quick Recap ###
The meeting focused on finalizing the presentation for ONNX’s application to become a PyTorch Foundation hosted project. Andreas shared the current slide deck and the group reviewed its content, suggesting changes to better highlight ONNX’s technical overlap with PyTorch, its role as a widely supported inference format, and the benefits of closer collaboration and visibility within the PyTorch ecosystem. The participants discussed how to frame the move from LF AI & Data to the PyTorch Foundation, emphasizing alignment, technical synergy, and potential for increased community engagement rather than governance comparisons. They refined messaging around ONNX’s stability, versioning, cross-platform support, and ecosystem, and considered adding more visual elements and a list of supporting vendors. The group also briefly touched on the need to update documentation related to experimental operators and the probabilistic programming working group. Overall, the meeting aimed to strengthen the narrative and clarity of the ONNX application presentation.
